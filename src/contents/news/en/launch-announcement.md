Welcome to the new ub-moji newsroom. This section gives us a cleaner way to share dataset releases, annotations, and community-facing updates without overloading the front page.

## What you'll find here

- Release notes for dataset revisions
- Calls for collaboration and workshop updates
- Short reports about benchmarks and evaluation plans

> We want updates to stay readable on phones, tablets, and laptops from the day they are published.

### Why Markdown

Markdown keeps editorial work lightweight while still letting us publish structured content.

```text
title: Small update
summary: One-line overview
```

Future posts will use this format to stay consistent across languages.
